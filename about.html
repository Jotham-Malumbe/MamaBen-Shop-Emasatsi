About MamaBen's Shop

Your trusted local shop in Emasatsi, Kakamega County

Our Story

MamaBen's Shop was founded with one simple goal — to provide fresh, quality daily essentials to families in Emasatsi and surrounding areas in Kakamega County at fair prices.

What started as a small family kiosk has grown into a trusted name because we focus on freshness, honesty, and convenience. We understand the needs of local families and work hard to serve you better every day.
